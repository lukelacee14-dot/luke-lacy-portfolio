About Me

Who I Am

I'm Luke Lacy, a final-year Bachelor of Sport and Exercise Science student at Swinburne University of Technology, currently completing an elite-level internship with the Richmond Football Club as a Performance Analyst.

I'm passionate about two things: how exercise science can improve the lives of everyday people, and how data and performance analysis can be used as a genuine competitive tool in elite sport. I'm driven to make an impact and deliver results through evidence-based, data-driven work.

Beyond sport science, I have a strong interest in building technology — from interactive R dashboards and Shiny apps to fully custom web and mobile applications — that makes data accessible and actionable.

Career Goals

  • Work in high-performance sport as a performance analyst or sport scientist
  • Develop data-driven tools that give teams and athletes a competitive edge
  • Bridge the gap between exercise science research and real-world application
  • Contribute to athlete wellbeing and long-term performance outcomes
